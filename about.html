Hello!

Venting Spaces LLP is an online wellness startup that aims to help Singaporeans vent healthily.

Our goal is to make these services accessible and convenient for everyone. We hope to provide virtual companionship and a community to encourage your growth.

More than 11 million appointments made with GPs here annually are a result of stress-related illnesses. 160,000 people are admitted to public hospitals as patients of stress-related illnesses every year, with annual medical costs at around US$931 million. 74% wish that they could spend more times with their families and loved ones. 52% said that they feel stuck in a daily routine that they are unable to get out of. The Straits Times

We aim to be accessible, affordable and transparent.

Our vision is to lessen the stigma of mental health issues by showing that caring for one's health does not require a diagnosis. It can come in the form of self-care, regular wellbeing-checks, or reminding oneself that it is okay to shoot for the moon and land among the stars. Gaining control of the steering wheel of your life can be hard, but it is possible.

We currently only provide online counselling but are looking forward to conducting physical fitness classes by the end of 2021.

We, the people behind this project, have gone through counselling and know people who have faced mental health troubles.
