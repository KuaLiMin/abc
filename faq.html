Here are some frequently asked questions. If you don't find your question here, you can reach us via the contact page.
What is Venting Spaces?

We are a wellness platform providing online counselling and fitness training (coming soon!). Our goal is to be a virtual companion for Singaporeans. We place emphasis on being accessible, affordable and transparent.

Why Counselling with Venting Spaces?

The best way to answer this is to tell you that the people behind this project have gone through counselling and know people who have faced mental health troubles too. We have experienced what life is like when the going gets tough and want to alleviate some of the pressure. Venting Spaces will not be successful based on profit, but on whether the process of healing and improving easier for those who choose us.

Are your counsellors certified?

Yes, we are very strict in our hiring and require our counsellors to have at least 1-year of work experience. All of them will have to be pursuing or have obtained a Masters Degree in Counselling. All our counsellors undergo strict supervision.

Upon booking, a report card will be emailed to you. It includes all the relevant information about the counsellor (e.g. education, work experience).

Who is Venting Spaces for?

We are for everyone and we cannot emphasise this enough. Our target audience is youths but if you don't belong to that category you are still able to book a session with us.

Whether or not you have depression/anxiety etc, mental health, regardless of demographic, it is something that we should always invest in. It’s good to have an avenue with a professional available and to talk about both good and bad happenings in order to keep on track. Remember, you’ll never know when something takes a toll on your mental health - speaking from personal experience!

How much are the sessions?

The more experienced the counsellors, the higher the chargeable rates will be. However, as we mentioned that we want to keep things affordable, the cap per hour is $80/hr and the lowest chargeable amount is $45/hr.

Our counsellors are all certified with work experience and undergo supervision in accordance to the Singapore's requirements.

If you're open to seeing a counsellor who is underqualified and still pursuing a counselling degree, contact us via the "contact us" tab to indicate. Do note that she will still be supervised by her lecturer. The rates will be $35/hr, flat charge for that.
